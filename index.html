Relationship semantics

Relationship labels describe source context.

Documented
A source records the relationship directly.
Attributed
A claim, quote, or position is attributed to a named source.
Alleged
A source presents an assertion that requires context.
Co-mentioned
Entities appear together in source evidence.
Curated
Editors added or reviewed a relationship for navigation.

Frequency is not corroboration.

Each relationship should be interpreted in the context of its underlying source.

  • Corrections are public.
